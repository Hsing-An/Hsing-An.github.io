This is one of my most representative works: a contemporary recorder piece blending jazz, electronic music, and Japanese folk-inspired elements.  
It received Second Prize and the Best Composition Award at Open Recorder Days Amsterdam 2023.
 <!--more-->

---
In this piece, I used the DAW [Reason](https://www.reasonstudios.com/?srsltid=AfmBOopOlDXSckGj6--NEWvOwjHG2pdTewFIFo40HpLlqW3qNLOWqujq) as my creative tool. Inspired by odd-meter music, I experimented with an uneven seven-beat rhythmic structure.  
In live performance, the piece combines live looping with a pre-programmed track. The recorder sound is also routed into the computer, where effects open up a wider range of timbral possibilities.

>Polygatari - 祭り(Matsuri)
>Monogatari（物語）是日文的”故事”，如同monophony進化（？）成polyphony一樣，我也想讓我的monogatari進化。  
>Matsuri（祭り）則是慶典。  

>Polygatari系列的第三作，大概就是發生在滿月的城下的祭典了吧，這裡的人步履蹣跚，卻又同時輕巧，他穿著白色變形蟲襯衫，他穿著羽織，他被拘束著，卻又自由。  
>而且他很帥！
