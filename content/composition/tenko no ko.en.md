**RADWIMPS' "Is There Still Anything That Love Can Do?" is one of the theme songs from the animated film *Weathering with You*. This version is arranged for recorder quartet.**

The arrangement preserves the original song's **long, narrative melodic line** while reworking the harmonic and accompaniment layers among the four parts.

In arranging the piece, I focused on the recorder's **transparency** and **singing quality**, hoping to present the original song's gradually building **emotional tension** and sense of hope within a more compact ensemble setting.

```
 Disconsonant Recorder Quartet:
 Hsing-An Lee
 Wen-Hong Jiang
 Chih-Yu Su
 Cheng-Yun Wang
 Composer: RADWIMPS
 Arrangement: Hsing-An Lee
```
