**LiSA's "Gurenge" is the opening theme song of the anime *Demon Slayer*. This version is arranged for recorder quartet.**

While keeping the original song's **clear melodic profile** and **forward drive**, the arrangement redistributes the melody, accompaniment, and rhythmic functions across the ensemble.

The arrangement makes use of the recorder ensemble's **clear layers of sound** and **agile articulation**, aiming to present the original song's strong **rhythmic character** and **dramatic tension**.
