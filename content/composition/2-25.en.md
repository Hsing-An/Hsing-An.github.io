**A short piece written for recorder quintet**, imitating the lively and energetic atmosphere of **Chinese New Year**.

Blending **fantasy colors** with **folk-inspired musical language**, the piece uses strong rhythmic patterns and **non-functional harmony** to create a sound world with a sense of **Eastern mystery** and **celebration**.

```
 Disconsonant Recorder Quartet:
 Hsing-An Lee
 Wen-Hong Jiang
 Kai-Li Chang
 Fu-Hong Liu
 Cheng-Yun Wang
 Composer: Hsing-An Lee
```
