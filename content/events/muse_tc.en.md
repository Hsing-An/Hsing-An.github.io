# Musesound is heading to Taichung!

My chamber partners and I, [Musesound Recorder Quartet](https://www.facebook.com/p/沐聲木笛四重奏Musesound-Recorder-Quartet-61566565385076/), will be giving a concert in Taichung.  
Since the other three members are all from Taichung, this is almost a hometown concert for them, which should feel quite relaxed.  
As the only member from Taitung, what I am most looking forward to is probably the celebratory barbecue afterward.

Musesound has been one of the projects I have spent the most time on recently. Come listen if you are interested!
