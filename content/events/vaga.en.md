# I am playing at a music festival!

I will be heading to Tainan with my band Long Time Don't Jam for [Vagabond Festival](https://www.vagabondfest.com)!  
We will perform several original works in fusion styles around hip-hop, R&B, and Bossa Nova, along with a few covers and some fresh new material.

This will be my first time performing on a music festival stage, so I am a little nervous.  
I have two performances that day (Oct. 18):

- 16:00 [Triggerman Beat Station](https://www.vagabondfest.com/pages/triggerman)
- 21:10 [Xiaoximen Stage](https://www.vagabondfest.com/pages/triggerman)

Come by if you are interested! [Tickets are available here](https://www.vagabondfest.com/pages/tickets)
