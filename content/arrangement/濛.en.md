This work was completed with classmates from a university elective course, using **alternative electronic music** as its stylistic direction.  
The piece takes a **synth bass** as its central motive, exploring the **flow and suspension** of the music.  
It describes the boundary between "**hazy**" and "**clear**" through sound.
