## This work was selected as the official station-entry music for Taichung MRT Shui-an Temple Station.

The station is connected to a Taiwanese Daoist temple; after leaving the platform, one can see red lanterns and the nearby Shui-an Temple. We decided to sample folk-like melodies from **traditional bayin music**, combining guzheng and suona-like timbres with a relaxed **Bossa Nova** accompaniment, adding a touch of temple culture to the modern urban setting.

The **suona-like sound** was sampled by us directly at a temple, which became one of the small details of the work.
