**Louis Andriessen's *ENDE* is a contemporary work for recorder**, with a direct and concentrated musical language. Its clear **sense of rhythm** focuses the music on even the smallest unstable changes.

In performance, I focused on **rhythmic stability** and **clarity of sound**, keeping the articulation precise so that the tension of the piece can emerge naturally.

```
 Hsing-An Lee Recorder Recital in Hsinchu, Taiwan
 Recorder: Hsing-An Lee
```
