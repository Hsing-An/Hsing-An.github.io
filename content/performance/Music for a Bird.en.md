**Hans-Martin Linde's *Music for a Bird* is a contemporary recorder work**, using **Extended technique vocabulary** and bird-like sound gestures. Rather than relying only on melody, the piece presents different bird calls through changes in **timbre** and ways of producing sound.

```
 Hsing-An Lee Recorder Recital in Hsinchu, Taiwan
 Recorder: Hsing-An Lee
```
