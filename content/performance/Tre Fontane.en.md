**Istanpitta "Tre Fontane" is a medieval instrumental dance.**  
It is preserved in the **British Library manuscript Add MS 29987**, an important fourteenth-century Italian source containing secular and sacred music. The manuscript also includes **fifteen monophonic instrumental dances** known as istanpitte, among which "Tre Fontane" appears.

Tre Fontane means **"three fountains."** What survives of the work is its title and original single-line melody, so modern performances often add various accompanying instruments, leaving performers considerable room for interpretation.
